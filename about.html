Who We Are

COMPANY OVERVIEW

As a private research organization which focuses on garnering support towards the ultimate humanitarian mission of space colonization, Galactic Academy has been always focusing on domesticating space towards every single person of the world. Galactic Academy consists of members from all over the Earth, as our organization is based on the peoples’ contribution towards a crucial and significant mission of extraterrestrial travel. We believe that with the support of more individuals towards the construction of private space companies to execute interstellar missions towards foreign celestial bodies, humanity will be able to salvage itself from the inevitable crisis of overpopulation, natural resource depletions, and unsustainable energy.

Galactic Academy was founded by CEO Kenzo Eugenio Tjandra under the impression of these humanitarian crises, implying an objective to innovate aerospace engineering as efficiently as possible. Space exploration and colonization unequivocally consumes a multifarious amount of resources and energy, where it is also undoubtedly time consuming to achieve successful missions which will revolutionize the aeronautical progress of a multiplanetary civilization as a whole. We investigated that funding for space exploration has declined by the government, while private space companies are the new assets for space exploration. However, most individuals are mainly lacking the courage to invest in such agencies, which will irrevocably determine the future of space exploration and its entirety. By exposing the importance and significance of these efforts of aerospace engineering towards the fate of humanity, we urge to restore the courage of our audiences to secure the mission of colonizing outer space.

PURPOSE

Our purpose as an advocacy and scientific organization is to highlight the importance of space exploration for humanity’s imminent future, where natural problems might be the eventual demise of the human race. We want to enact ourselves as an advisor for many people both within the general and scientific community, where our mission is to implement the courage to invest in long term space exploration missions. Every single person within the planet possesses the ability to support our cause, where it is our goal to eliminate the sense of doubt towards the scientific development in aeronautics.
