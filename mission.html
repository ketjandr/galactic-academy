OVERPOPULATION

Overpopulation as an internal crisis within humanity is highly eminent given the exponential rate of human birth within the 21st century. It is predicted that Earth would reach a staggering population of 9.8 billion humans in 2050 alone, which could potentially cause poverty, malnutrition, and starvation.

RESOURCE DELPLETION

The depletion of natural resources remains a predominant issue due to the excessive use of natural resources given how Earth has a limited amount of them. Predictions dictate that given the current rate of natural resource consumption, fossil fuels would run out in 2060.

UNSUSTAINABLE ENERGY

Unsustainable energy is a natural crisis which is caused by implementing unclean and non-renewable energy as a reliant for power supply. The usage of these unsustainable energies would cause atrocities such as global warming and climate change.
