OVERCOMING SPACE

People may fear space exploration for various reasons. From contracting astrophobia to the doubt of dedicating long term investments to extraterrestrial travel itself, space is undeniably scary for some and perhaps many. However, there is no logical reason to fear outer space itself, as it is scientifically beneficial towards human civilization, where the possibilities of countless technological innovations are endless. Overcoming the fear to possess a curious mind to explore space might just be halting the world from a rampant development within aerospace engineering, where it is up to every single individual to salvage the future generations of mankind.
