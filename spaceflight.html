Constructing a Multiplanetary Society

WHAT IS A MULTIPLANETARY SOCIETY

A multiplanetary civilization possesses the ability to produce large quantities of energy in a short period of time, retaining a sustainable amount of resources for its large magnitude of population growth. This scale of civilization most basically refers to a society which has colonized multiple planets, able to extract natural resources and construct infrastructures in foreign extraterrestrial soil. The involvement of the colonization of multiple planets requires a thorough understanding upon the multiplanetary system consisting of a star and its orbiting planets. Extractions of valuable information from other planets including Mars and Venus may lead humanity into the path towards a multiplanetary order.

EXTRATERRESTRIAL TRAVEL

The revolutionary notion of extraterrestrial travel is a revelation towards human society, as only a small number of 566 astronauts and cosmonauts in humanity’s history have left the Earth’s exosphere into the vacuums of outer space. Travelling to outer space requires an exceptional understanding of overcoming the environmental conditions of space. These include adapting without a gravitational force, coping with freezing temperatures, and avoiding micro meteoroids and asteroids alike. However, extraterrestrial travel conducted frequently will allow humans to adapt the environmental fluctuations of outer space and obtain a rewarding premise towards an advanced space civilization.

DOMESTIC SPACEFLIGHT

Launching domestic passengers into the depths of outer space is one of the most ambitious objectives within the ongoing project of space exploration and colonization. Once advancements within the aerospace industry deems it as a plausible mission to send humans into extraterrestrial bounds, populating a planet after being mostly terraformed will allow for the beginning of a multiplanetary society.

ADVANCED COLONIZATION

The space industrial revolution where a multiplanetary civilization is fulfilled allows for the human race to achieve an advanced level of extraterrestrial colonization within the district of the solar system. An advanced extraterrestrial colony will be able to harness the energy of the sun, with megastructures such as Dyson Sphere, and utilize the obtained resources to send more spacecraft and artificial satellites to exoplanets up to light years away from the solar system. Aspirations to transform humanity into an advanced extraterrestrial colony is ultimately persistent for many astronomers and scientists, as theoretical technologies including stellar engines and mega skyhooks are able to be implemented to innovate space exploration.
